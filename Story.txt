Arin lifted the microchip—untouched, unharmed.
But behind him, footsteps echoed softly.
He turned to find Loop removing their mask.
A face he never expected—his old protégé.
Eyes filled with a mix of brilliance and regret.

“They needed to test the system,” Loop said quietly.
“And you’re the only one who could solve it.”
Arin pocketed the chip, unsure whether to arrest or protect.
The game had changed forever.
But the code would haunt him for years.
