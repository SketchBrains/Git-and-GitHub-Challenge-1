Arin wrote the four missing hours: 02, 05, 07, 11.
The order felt important.
He checked them against archives of Loop’s past crimes.
Every incident matched one of those hours.
Loop was retracing their own history.

Each crime file had a single highlighted sentence.
When combined, they formed a riddle:
“What breaks without sound but screams in numbers?”
Arin knew the answer immediately.
A digital code.
