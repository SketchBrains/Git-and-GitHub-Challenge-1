Arin assembled the gears and turned them slowly.
As they spun, they projected numbers through slits on the wall.
But the numbers kept shifting.
This puzzle wasn’t static—it reacted to direction.
Clockwise gave one set; counterclockwise gave another.

He rotated them alternatingly.
The projection finally settled on a four-word phrase:
“Check the missing hours.”
Arin checked the tower clock.
Hours 2, 5, 7, and 11 were scraped off.
